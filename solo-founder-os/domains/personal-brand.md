# Domain: Personal Brand

id: domain/personal-brand
version: 1.0.0

## Rules

- Preserve the personal voice, positioning and visual identity of the site.
- Do not add unnecessary frameworks, build tools or dependencies.
- Treat copy, SEO, links, images and public-facing claims carefully.
- Do not invent biography, achievements, partnerships, investment history or project status.
- Keep changes lightweight and easy to review.
