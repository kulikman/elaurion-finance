# Domain: CRM

id: domain/crm
version: 1.0.0

## Rules

- Do not invent customer records, statuses, roles, permissions, email workflows or CRM lifecycle states.
- Preserve audit trails and access-control logic.
- Treat customer data, invoices, bookings, company records and email delivery as sensitive.
- Stop and ask before changing role checks, protected files, email delivery, migrations or finance-related flows.
